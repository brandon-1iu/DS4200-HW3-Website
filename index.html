About Me

I've always been fascinated by using technology to solve financial and business challenges, which led me to pursue my current studies. My academic journey has been enriched by experiences at Northeastern's campuses in London, Bay Area, and Boston, which have shaped my global perspective and strengthened my passion for this field.

These interests help me maintain a balanced lifestyle and provide me with opportunities to explore different cultures and perspectives. I believe that a well-rounded approach to life is essential for personal growth and development.

In the future, I hope to apply my skills in data analysis, financial innovation, and international business to make meaningful contributions to the industry. I'm particularly interested in roles such as Business Analyst, Technical Consultant, Data Science Intern, Data Analyst, and Business Intelligence Analyst. I'm excited about the possibilities that lie ahead and am committed to continuing my learning journey.
